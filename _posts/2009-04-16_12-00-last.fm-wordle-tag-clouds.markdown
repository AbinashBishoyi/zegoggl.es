Creating Last.fm tag clouds with wordle

In order to play a bit with the [last.fm API](http://www.last.fm/api) and [sinatra](http://www.sinatrarb.com)
I created a little microapp which lets you visualise your listening history (recent tracks, top tracks...)
in a beautiful word cloud, using the well-known [wordle](http://www.wordle.net/) service.

Check it out: [lastfm-wordle.heroku.com](http://lastfm-wordle.heroku.com).
